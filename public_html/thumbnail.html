Bulbasuar


(フシギダネ)


Bulbasaur found in grasslands and forests throughout the Kanto region. However, due to Bulbasaur's status as starter Pokémon, it is hard to come by in the wild and generally found under the ownership of a Trainer.

Squirtle


(ゼニガメ)


Squirtle is a small Pokémon that resembles a light-blue turtle. Squirtle is scarce in the wild, although it can be found around small ponds and lakes.

Charmander


(ヒトカゲ)


Charmander is a bipedal, reptilian Pokémon with a primarily orange body and blue eyes. Charmander can be found in hot, mountainous areas. However, it is found far more often in the ownership of Trainers.

Eevee


(イーブイ)


Eevee is a small, mammalian, quadrupedal Pokémon with primarily brown fur. Eevee is rarely found in the wild and is mostly only found in cities and towns. However, it is said to have an irregularly shaped genetic structure that is easily influenced by its environment.

Charizard


(リザードン)


Charizard is a large, dragon-like Pokémon that differs greatly from its pre-evolved form. The fire on Charizard's tail will change to a bluish-white color if it is furious. Given its dragon-like appearance. Charizard are noble and will not turn the full power of their flame on opponents .

Mew


(ミュウ)


Mew is a pink, bipedal Pokémon with mammalian features. Mew is said to have the DNA of every single Pokémon contained within its body, allowing Mew to learn and use any attack. Making him the most valuble addition.
